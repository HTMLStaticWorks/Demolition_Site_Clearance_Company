Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Demolition Questions

Find swift answers regarding local permitting durations, recycling standards, and abatement logistics.

How long does a commercial demolition permit take?

Usually between 2 to 4 weeks depending on the municipal council and environmental soil/asbestos assessment cycles.

What happens to reclaimed structural steel?

We process it immediately on site using excavator magnets, clean it, and ship it directly to smelting mills for 100% recycling.

Do you handle hazardous asbestos mitigation?

Yes, our demolition crews are fully EPA licensed and trained to secure, isolate, and legally dispose of all hazardous asbestos structures.
