Specialized Equipment

Engineered High-Reach Demolition

For high-density urban environments, structural disassembly requires maximum control. Our heavy excavator fleet features high-reach arms equipped with demolition shears and concrete pulverizers, allowing surgical dismantling from the top down.

Pinpoint Shear Attachments

Hydraulic steel cutters that snap beams and structural steel safely at high elevations.

Cabin Monitoring Systems

Real-time digital camera systems mapping the destruction field directly to operator cabins.

Integrated Dust Suppression

Pressurized water foggers mounted directly on the mechanical arm to bind dust at the contact point.

Sustainability

98% Material Diversion & Recovery

We process structural demolition materials on-site, converting potential landfill debris into valuable construction assets.

Concrete Crushing

On-site mobile crushing plants process structural concrete rubble, transforming it into certified aggregate for road bases and new foundations.

Metal Recovery
